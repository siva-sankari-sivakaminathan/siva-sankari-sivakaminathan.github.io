About Me

Hi , I'm Siva Sankari Sivakaminathan,a data science student and a whirlwind of energy disguised as a mom to a curious toddler.

My journey to data science has been an exciting adventure filled with discovery! It all began in the world of software testing, where I thrived on ensuring smooth software operation through meticulous attention to detail. This experience ignited a passion for uncovering patterns and fostering creative solutions, skills that perfectly translate to the world of data science. Motherhood arrived, adding a whole new dimension of data analysis! Juggling nap schedules, deciphering toddler giggles (sometimes cries!), and optimizing meal plans all require a keen eye for detail, adaptability, and a creative approach to problem-solving.


Now, as a Master's student at the University of Liverpool in Data Science and Communication, I'm thrilled to be combining my technical expertise with the ability to communicate insights effectively. My diverse background allows me to see data science from a unique perspective. The focus on data quality from my testing days is invaluable, and motherhood has instilled in me the importance of clear communication and efficiency. This translates into building data-driven solutions that are not only effective but also easily understood by stakeholders. I'm constantly learning and exploring the frontiers of data science, eager to contribute to projects that make a positive impact!
